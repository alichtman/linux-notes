### Extension Settings


## Firefox Settings

Go to `about:config`.

### Disable zoom on Ctrl+Mouse Scroll

Set `mousewheel.with_control.action` to `0`.

### Disable history scrolling on Alt+Scroll

Set `mousewheel.with_alt.action` to `0`
